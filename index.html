About Our Church

At New Revival Baptist Church in Arepo, we are a vibrant community of believers dedicated to experiencing and sharing God's transforming love. Our mission is to create an environment where everyone can encounter Jesus Christ and grow in their faith journey.

We believe in the power of worship, fellowship, and service to strengthen our relationship with God and with one another. Join us as we seek to make disciples and impact our community for Christ.
